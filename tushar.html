What is a Virtual AI Doctor Assistant?

A Virtual AI Doctor Assistant is an intelligent digital tool designed to provide general medical guidance, health-related information, and wellness advice. It assists users in understanding symptoms, offering first-level health insights, and promoting well-being.

This AI-powered assistant is not a replacement for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. However, it can be a helpful first step in navigating your health concerns and directing you toward appropriate care.
